Our Services

Medical Checkups

Comprehensive medical checkup services to ensure your well-being.


Physical Examination

A thorough physical examination is conducted by a healthcare provider. This includes checking vital signs such as blood pressure, heart rate, respiratory rate, and temperature. The provider also examines various body systems, including the heart, lungs, abdomen, and skin.

Cholesterol Levels

Blood tests are often performed to measure cholesterol levels, including total cholesterol, LDL (low-density lipoprotein) cholesterol (the "bad" cholesterol), and HDL (high-density lipoprotein) cholesterol (the "good" cholesterol). Abnormal cholesterol levels can indicate a risk of heart disease.

Cancer Screenings

Depending on age, gender, and risk factors, medical checkups may include cancer screenings such as mammograms for breast cancer, Pap smears for cervical cancer, prostate-specific antigen (PSA) tests for prostate cancer, and colonoscopies for colorectal cancer

Surgery

Comprehensive Surgical services to ensure your well-being.


Neurosurgery

Neurosurgeons specialize in the surgical treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord. They perform surgeries for brain tumors, aneurysms, spinal cord injuries, and neurological conditions.

Orthopedic Surgery

Orthopedic surgeons specialize in the treatment of musculoskeletal conditions. They perform procedures such as joint replacements, arthroscopic surgeries, fracture repairs, and spinal surgeries to alleviate pain and improve mobility.

Cardiothoracic Surgery

Cardiothoracic surgeons focus on heart and chest surgeries. They perform coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve repair/replacement, lung surgeries, and procedures to treat congenital heart defects.
